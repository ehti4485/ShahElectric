⚡ Our Story

About VoltEdge Power

Lighting up homes and lives for over 18 years. We are a locally owned electricity distribution company committed to keeping over 1,000 families powered, safe, and informed.

⚡ Who We Are

More Than a Utility — We Are Your Neighbors

VoltEdge Power Distribution was established in 2005 with a single mission: to provide affordable and reliable electricity to underserved residential communities. What began as a small operation covering a few streets has grown into a full-scale distribution network serving over 1,000 homes.

We are a privately managed electricity distributor operating under applicable national regulatory frameworks. Our team of certified engineers, meter readers, field technicians, and customer service staff work together every single day to ensure uninterrupted power supply to every connected household.

Unlike large utility corporations, we operate locally — which means faster response times, direct accountability, and a genuine connection to the people we serve. Every complaint matters. Every outage is urgent. Every customer is known to us by name, not just by a meter number.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

VoltEdge Power Distribution ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our customers. This Privacy Policy applies to all customers, visitors to our website, and individuals who interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide electricity distribution services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, CNIC number, address, phone number, and email address of the primary account holder.
  • Property Information: Physical address of the connected property, meter number, and connection type.
  • Consumption Data: Monthly electricity meter readings, usage history, and load patterns.
  • Billing Information: Payment history, outstanding amounts, and billing cycle data. We do not store credit/debit card details.
  • Communication Records: Records of complaints, fault reports, and service requests submitted by customers.
  • Website Data: If you use our website, we may collect standard web analytics data such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent — via cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purposes of providing and improving our electricity distribution service, including:

  • Processing your monthly electricity bill accurately
  • Responding to outage reports, complaints, and service requests
  • Maintaining accurate records for regulatory compliance
  • Notifying you of planned maintenance or service interruptions
  • Improving our infrastructure and service quality

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data may only be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With regulatory authorities (e.g., NEPRA, WAPDA) as required by applicable law
  • With authorized contractors who assist in maintenance or metering — bound by strict confidentiality agreements
  • When legally required by a court order or government authority

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. All customer records are stored in secured systems with access restricted to authorized personnel only. Physical records are kept in locked premises. We review our security practices regularly.

However, no method of data storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

As a customer of VoltEdge Power Distribution, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your personal information held by us
  • The right to request corrections to inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your data upon termination of service (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • The right to raise a complaint about how your data is handled

7. Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used purely for functional and analytical purposes. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of our website may not function correctly.

8. Data Retention

Customer records including billing history and personal data are retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with applicable tax and regulatory requirements. After this period, records are securely destroyed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any material changes will be communicated to active customers via written notice or posting on our office notice board. Continued use of our services after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
